How to play?

  • The Computer will generate a random number between range 1-100.
  • You have to match your guessed number with the number generated by computer.
  • If your guessed number is lesser than the generated number it will show "Your guess is low" and if your guessed number is higher than generated number it will show "your guess is High".
  • If your guessed number match with the generated number you will win the game.
  • You have 5 attempts to guess the number in hard mode and 10 attempts in easy mode you have 10 attempts.
